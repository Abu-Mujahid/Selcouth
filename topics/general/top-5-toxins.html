Top 5 Toxins in Our Environment

Aug 30, 2025 •
  1. Aluminium: literally everywhere — aluminium cookware, deodorants, and even the air we breathe. Linked in literature to Alzheimer’s and other neurodegenerative diseases.
  2. Glyphosate: “you’re not gluten intolerant; you’re glyphosate intolerant.” Most commonly used herbicide worldwide; damages gut lining and can travel to the brain.
  3. Fluoride: in water and toothpaste; described as a neurotoxin.
  4. Wi‑Fi and 5G: potentially destructive to our cells — often without obvious symptoms.
  5. Skincare and household products: many contain endocrine disruptors.
