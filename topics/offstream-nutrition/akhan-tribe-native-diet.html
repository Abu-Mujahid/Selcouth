Native Diet Example: The Akhan Tribeswoman

Aug 31, 2025 •

When faced with the question of what humans should eat, there are many differing opinions out there. Meat? Fruit? Grains? Seeds? Vegetables? Somewhere in between?

How do we find the truth? Maybe we should look at what humans ate before their dietary habits were corrupted by those in the Western world with vested interests.

Take this native Akhan tribeswoman, for example. For 85 years she lived deep in the forests of Northern Thailand. Her tribe was so remote, that for 40+ years she didn’t know what country she lived in, or that other races of people even existed.

No one told the Akhan what to eat. They worked it out themselves over thousands of years living in harmony with nature. Like every other native tribe that has ever existed, the Akhan ate animals, vegetables, nuts, seeds, and fruit.

Our forefathers deduced what the optimal human diet was through generations of trial and error. It was determined entirely by what was available, seasonal, local, fresh, and edible.

Humans have eaten this way since the dawn of time, in harmony with nature, whilst remaining largely free from disease. So why do we think they somehow got it all wrong?
