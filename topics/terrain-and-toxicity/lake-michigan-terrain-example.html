Lake Michigan: A Terrain Contrast

Aug 31, 2025 •

A perfect example of the terrain theory in nature. On one side of Lake Michigan is crystal clear blue water. On the other side, it is filthy brown, reeks of sewage, & full of dead fish. The clear side is free of dead fish as it is the environment that initiated disease & death.
