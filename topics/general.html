Top 5 Toxins in Our Environment

Posted in General • Aug 30, 2025

  1. Aluminium: literally everywhere—aluminium cookware, deodorants, and even the air we breathe. Linked in literature to Alzheimer’s and other neurodegenerative conditions.
  2. Glyphosate: “you’re not gluten intolerant; you’re glyphosate intolerant.” Most commonly used herbicide worldwide; can impair gut lining and may reach the brain.
  3. Fluoride: in water and toothpaste; described as a neurotoxin in various sources.
  4. Wi‑Fi and 5G: potential cellular stressors—often unnoticed in daily life.
  5. Skincare and household products: many contain endocrine disruptors; check labels and simplify where possible.
